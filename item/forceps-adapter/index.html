Forceps adapter

Add to Cart Go to Cart

Info

Forceps adapter for safe feet/hands replacement or doll restringing.
Using forceps without adapter can damage doll parts.

The adapter is designed for use with Hemostatic Forceps Straight 5.5” (14cm) or 6.25” (16cm)
The adapter is 3D printed from PLA plastic of random color.

Follow instructions to replace foot or hand:

  1. Insert forceps in adapter and unlock it
  2. Fix doll’s body between your legs or with the help of someone
  3. Pull out the foot or hand by 5-7cm (requires considerable effort)
  4. Use forceps to fix strings and safely put adapter in joint
  5. Replace feet or hand
  6. Unlock forceps while holding feet/hand and remove adapter from joint
